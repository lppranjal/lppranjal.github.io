Project Overview
Purpose: To provide an online resume/portfolio showcasing my skills, work experience, and educational background.
Sections:
About Me: Brief introduction with a photo and a summary of my background.
Work Experience: Detailed table listing companies, job durations, and the projects/technologies involved.
Education: Compact table format for qualifications and relevant courses.
Contact: Key contact details and direct links to my social profiles.
Features
Responsive Design:
Ensures optimal viewing on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Table-Based Experience & Education:
Presents information in a concise, easily scannable format.

Simple Navigation Menu:
Quick links to About, Work Experience, Education, and Contact sections.

Social Media Links:
Buttons for GitHub and LinkedIn, opening in new tabs.
